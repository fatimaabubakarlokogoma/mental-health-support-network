# Mental Health Support Network\n\n## Overview\n\nA blockchain-based anonymous mental health support platform that connects patients with therapists while rewarding participation through tokenized therapy sessions and peer counseling. The platform ensures privacy through cryptographic anonymity while incentivizing mental wellness engagement.\n\n## 🧠 Real World Inspiration\n\nInspired by **Mindstrong Health**, which uses blockchain technology to securely store mental health data while rewarding patients with tokens for completing therapy sessions and wellness activities. This addresses the global mental health crisis affecting 970+ million people worldwide.\n\n## ✨ Key Features\n\n### For Patients\n- **Anonymous Matching**: Cryptographically anonymous patient-therapist pairing\n- **Token Rewards**: Earn wellness tokens for session completion and progress milestones\n- **Peer Support Networks**: Community-driven support groups with privacy protection\n- **Progress Tracking**: Encrypted progress records with patient-controlled access\n- **Global Access**: Borderless mental health support regardless of location\n- **Affordable Care**: Reduced costs through token-based incentive systems\n\n### For Therapists\n- **Professional Network**: Verified credentials and reputation system\n- **Flexible Scheduling**: Set availability and session preferences\n- **Token Earnings**: Earn tokens for quality therapy sessions and patient outcomes\n- **Privacy Protection**: Maintain patient confidentiality through blockchain anonymity\n- **Outcome Tracking**: Monitor patient progress while preserving privacy\n- **Global Practice**: Serve patients worldwide through decentralized platform\n\n### For the Community\n- **Peer Counseling Rewards**: Token incentives for community support activities\n- **Mental Health Research**: Anonymous data contributions for research purposes\n- **Wellness Challenges**: Group activities and challenges with token rewards\n- **Crisis Support Network**: Emergency mental health response system\n- **Education Resources**: Mental health awareness and education programs\n\n## 🏗️ System Registration Phase\n1. **Patient Onboarding**: Anonymous registration with wellness assessment\n2. **Therapist Verification**: Credential verification and platform approval\n3. **Privacy Setup**: Cryptographic keys generated for anonymous communication\n4. **Preference Matching**: AI-driven compatibility assessment\n\n### 2. Therapy Sessions\n1. **Anonymous Matching**: Algorithm pairs patients with compatible therapists\n2. **Session Scheduling**: Encrypted booking system preserves anonymity\n3. **Secure Communication**: End-to-end encrypted therapy sessions\n4. **Progress Tracking**: Encrypted milestone tracking and goal setting\n5. **Token Rewards**: Automatic token distribution for session completion\n\n### 3. Community Support\n1. **Peer Networks**: Join anonymous support groups by condition/interest\n2. **Wellness Challenges**: Participate in mental health improvement activities\n3. **Crisis Support**: Emergency mental health intervention system\n4. **Resource Sharing**: Community-driven mental health resource library\n\n## 🛡️ Privacy & Security\n\n### Patient Privacy\n- **Zero-Knowledge Identity**:
